About Flatemate

Flatemate is a simple and intuitive platform for roommates to manage shared finances seamlessly. Track rent, payments, and household expenses in one place, ensuring transparency and easy coordination among all roommates.

The app is designed to simplify financial collaboration, providing a user-friendly interface for both admins and roommates. With Flatemate, you can stay on top of rent collection, expense sharing, and budgeting effortlessly, making shared living smoother and stress-free.
